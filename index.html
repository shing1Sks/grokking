Key insights
Weight decay = 1.0
Strong L2 pressure killed memorization in ~7,200 steps — forcing the network to find a compressed, generalizing solution.
250K parameters
Tiny model. The circuit that emerged implements discrete Fourier transforms — representing addition in the frequency domain.
70% unseen pairs
Trained on 2,822 examples. After grokking it generalizes perfectly to all 6,587 pairs it never saw during training.
